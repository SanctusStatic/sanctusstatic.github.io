About

StaticLine Logistics Group is a veteran-owned software company based in Ocala, Florida. We build beautiful, offline-first mobile applications for communities rooted in tradition, discipline, and daily practice.

Founded by a U.S. Army veteran, our mission is to create tools that serve focused communities with the quality and attention they deserve.

Sanctus
Traditional Latin Mass Companion

A free companion app for the Traditional Latin Mass. Sanctus provides a liturgical calendar, daily propers in Latin and English, prayer alarms, the Holy Rosary with full prayers for all three traditional mystery sets (Joyful, Sorrowful, and Glorious), Stations of the Cross, the Divine Mercy Chaplet, and fasting discipline reference — all designed with the reverence and beauty the traditional liturgy deserves. Fully offline. No account required. No data collected.
