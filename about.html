About Sanctus

Sanctus is a devotional companion app for the Traditional Latin Mass (Extraordinary Form of the Roman Rite), built for the faithful who love the ancient liturgy and wish to pray with it more deeply each day.

The app provides the full liturgical calendar for 2026 according to the 1962 Roman Rite, including daily propers ‚Äî Introit, Collect, Epistle, and Gospel ‚Äî in both Latin and English, fully offline. It also includes the Holy Rosary with full prayers in Latin and English, Stations of the Cross, the Divine Mercy Chaplet, sacred alarm sounds, and daily fasting guidance.

Sanctus is published by StaticLine Logistics Group LLC, a Florida limited liability company. Enrollment ID: 2MCMK78V64.

V

Fasting Guidance

1962 Discipline

The 1962 fasting discipline reflects the stricter pre-conciliar rules, including Ember Days, Vigil fasts, and the full Lenten fast. This information is provided for general reference and historical understanding.

1983 Discipline

The 1983 discipline reflects the current Code of Canon Law (canons 1249‚Äì1253), which requires fasting on Ash Wednesday and Good Friday, and abstinence from meat on Fridays of Lent.
