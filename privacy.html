Privacy Policy — Sanctus

Last updated: February 19, 2026  ·  StaticLine Logistics Group LLC

No Data Collected

Sanctus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information — including your alarm settings and prayer preferences — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage and never leaves it. We have no servers and no databases. We cannot see your data and we never will.

No Third-Party Services

Sanctus does not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any third-party services of any kind. The app operates entirely offline at runtime. No network requests are made while you use the app.

Notification Permissions

Sanctus requests iOS notification permissions solely to deliver sacred alarm sounds at times you configure. This permission is optional. No notification content is transmitted off your device.

No Account Required

Sanctus requires no account, login, email address, or personal information of any kind to use.

Children

Sanctus does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. The app contains no advertising and requires no account or personal data.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Because we collect no data, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in any material way.

Governing Law
