Privacy Policy — Sanctus App

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Sanctus is developed by StaticLine Logistics Group LLC.

Sanctus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information — including your alarm settings, prayer preferences, and examination of conscience entries — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

Sanctus does not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or third-party services of any kind. We have no servers and no databases. We cannot see your data and we never will.

Sanctus requests notification permissions solely to deliver alarm sounds at times you configure. No other device permissions are requested.

If you have questions, contact us at info@staticlinegroup.com.
